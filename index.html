About

Our Engineered Oak foors are made up of layers. The top layer is 100% natural oak, and other species on request. The bottom layer is also wood. In the middle is a core built from 5 to 7 layers of plywood that crisscross in different directions. Engineered construction creates a highly stable core that is less likely to expand, contract or shift when exposed to moisture, humidity and temperature. This makes engineered wood fooring a great option in rooms that are subject to moisture (like basements) or over concrete slab and radiant heating systems. Our solid Oak fooring is constructed with 100% hardwood and each plank is a single solid piece. At Deska Wooden Flooring, we source all of our solid hardwood in the Poland to support all workers and their fne craftsmanship, and ensure the highest possible quality. Our primary source for Oak wood is North Poland. Oak from this region is preferred for its consistent color, more refned grain, and better stability.

Deska Flooring’s solid Oak fooring is durable and reliable, making it a wonderful investment that will enhance the value of your home and provide beauty for a lifetime. Grades describe the expressive power of a wood foor. The careful selection and combination of knot content, grain and colour interplay add an entirely personal touch to each plank we select. We select three type of gradeing: nature, standard and rustic.

Our foors are fnished with SAICOS Coating Systems
